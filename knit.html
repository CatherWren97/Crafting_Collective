Learn to knit with RJ

On his channel RJ Knits, RJ Collett explores different ways of knitting, tecniques for knitters of all levels. He is a self proclaimed "author, knitter, and animator" and on his channel, he makes "videos you can wear". His videos are informative and easy to follow, and are good for anyone who wants to learn, or sharpen their kniting skills.

Beginner Knitting for Lefties

Since knitting uses both hands regularly, you might be wondering how it can be left or right handed. But despite the usage of both hands, which direction you knit in depends on which is your dominent hand. If you are left hand dominant, or simply find knitting with your right hand dominant too difficult, try with your left hand!
